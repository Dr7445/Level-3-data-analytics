Provide blended data in an appropriate format

 

For this assignment I sourced 2 datasets from Kaggle, the primary source is a dataset on student screen time and sleeping patterns, and the secondary dataset is  the UK mental health and screen time dataset also from Kaggle.

I created a shared Age_group column and summarised the secondary data via a pivot table,

However, since pivot tables are dynamic and not ideal for referencing in formulas like XLOOKUP I then pasted the pivot table into a separate worksheet as static values.

This allowed me to create a clean, fixed lookup table, ensuring accurate and reliable data blending when joining the national averages to the primary student dataset.


The final data clearly shows each students screen and sleep times against the national averages, thereby demonstrating blending and formatting of data from multiple sources.
